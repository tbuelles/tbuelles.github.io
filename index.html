About Me

I'm a Mathematician, Developer, Cyclist based in Pasadena, CA. I'm a Taussky–Todd Postdoctoral Scholar at Caltech working in Algebraic Geometry and String Theory. I received my PhD in Mathematics from ETH Zürich. My advisor was Prof. Pandharipande. Previously, I was an undergraduate at the University of Bonn in the group of Prof. Huybrechts. My current research focuses on enumerative geometry and various aspects of K3 surfaces and Calabi–Yau 3-folds.
