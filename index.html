About

I'm a Taussky–Todd Postdoctoral Scholar for Mathematics at Caltech and a Machine Learning Research Scientist at Pythia Labs. I work on geometric deep learning methods for generative and probabilistic modeling. I did my PhD at ETH Zürich in the group of Prof. Pandharipande. My thesis focuses on special symmetries of enumerative invariants derived from Calabi–Yau geometries in superstring theory. Previously, I studied at the University of Bonn where I worked with Prof. Huybrechts on algebraic cycles and motives of K3 surfaces.
