About Me

I am a Taussky–Todd Instructor at Caltech. I received my PhD in Mathematics from ETH Zürich. My advisor was Rahul Pandharipande. Previously, I was an undergraduate student at the University of Bonn in the group of Daniel Huybrechts. My research focuses on algebraic geometry with emphasize on enumerative geometry and various aspects of K3 surfaces and Calabi–Yau threefolds.
