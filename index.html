About

I'm a Taussky–Todd Postdoctoral Scholar for Mathematics at Caltech where I work on geometric deep learning and applications of AI/ML in mathematics and physics. I received my PhD '22 from ETH Zürich. My advisor was Prof. Pandharipande. My thesis focuses on special symmetries of enumerative invariants derived from Calabi–Yau geometries in superstring theory. Previously, I received my MSc '18 and BSc '16 from the University of Bonn where I worked with Prof. Huybrechts on algebraic cycles and motives of K3 surfaces.
