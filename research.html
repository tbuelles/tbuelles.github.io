Overview

My reserach is in algebraic geometry. I am interested in various aspects of Calabi--Yau spaces, derived categories, stability conditions, and moduli spaces. Recently, I have focussed on questions in the enumerative geometry of K3 surfaces and Calabi–Yau 3-folds with motivation from mirror symmetry and string theory.
